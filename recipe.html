Pasta

Ingredients:
  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
Preparation:
  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve pasta with sauce.

Fried Rice

Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chicken
Preparation:
  1. Cook rice and let it cool.
  2. Scramble eggs in a pan and set aside.
  3. Sauté vegetables and chicken in the same pan.
  4. Add rice and soy sauce, then mix everything together.

Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:
  • Broccoli
  • Bell Peppers
  • Carrots
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
Preparation:
  1. Chop vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic.
  3. Add vegetables and stir fry until tender.
  4. Drizzle with soy sauce before serving.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Eggs
  • Butter
Preparation:
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and cocoa powder in a bowl.
  2. Add eggs and melted butter, then mix until smooth.
  3. Pour batter into a greased cake pan.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes.

Pizza

Ingredients:
  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olives
Preparation:
  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add cheese and toppings of your choice.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
