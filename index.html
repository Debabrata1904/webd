Scrambled Eggs

To make scrambled eggs, you will need the following ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them together.
  2. Season the eggs with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour the eggs into the pan and let them cook for a few seconds.
  5. Using a spatula, gently stir the eggs until they are cooked to your desired consistency.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your scrambled eggs!
